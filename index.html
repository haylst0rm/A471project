Truth in Photography

About This Project

“Truth in Photography” is a series of satirical pictures, representing the debate of accuracy within the Photography community. The debate/theme is that photography is not truthful, and can be manipulated very easily in a variety of ways. The process of this project started with selecting highly-recognizable, historical photographs, and editing them through Photoshop and Procreate to produce an altered result. This result is very clear, adding to the intention of satire. Overall, one should view these pictures and observe every picture with curiosity to its ‘truthfulness’.
